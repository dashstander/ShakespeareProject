  VARRO. Calls my lord?
  VARRO. So please you, we will stand and watch your pleasure.
  VARRO. My lord?
  VARRO. CLAUDIO. Did we, my lord?
  VARRO. No, my lord, I saw nothing.
  VARRO. CLAUDIO. It shall be done, my lord.             Exeunt.
