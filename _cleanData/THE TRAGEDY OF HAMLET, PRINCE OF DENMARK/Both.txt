  Both. Arm'd, my lord.
  Both. My lord, from head to foot.
  Both. Longer, longer.
  Both. Ay, by heaven, my lord.  
  Both. My lord, we will not.
  Both. We'll wait upon you.  
  Both. We will, my lord.                       Exeunt they two.
  Both. We will haste us.
                                               Exeunt Gentlemen.
